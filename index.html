01

Pani Puri

Pani puri is a popular Indian street food snack that consists of small, crispy, hollow puri shells filled with a mixture of spicy and tangy potato, chickpeas, onions, and chutney, and then dunked into a bowl of mint-flavored water, also known as pani. The combination of crispy puri, tangy filling, and refreshing pani makes it a favorite among many food lovers in India and beyond.

02

Samosas

Samosa is a popular Indian snack that is widely enjoyed all over the country and beyond. It is a triangular or cone-shaped pastry filled with a spicy mixture of potatoes, peas, onions, and various spices, and then deep-fried until crispy and golden brown. Samosas are usually served with tamarind or mint chutney, and are a common sight at parties, weddings, and other festive occasions.

03

Pav Bhaji

Pav bhaji is a popular Indian street food dish that originated in Mumbai. It consists of a thick and spicy vegetable curry, called bhaji, that is made with a blend of mashed vegetables such as potatoes, peas, onions, tomatoes, and spices.Pav bhaji is a favorite among vegetarians and non-vegetarians alike and is often enjoyed as a filling and satisfying meal.
